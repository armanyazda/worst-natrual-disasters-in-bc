Natural disasters in BC

Most famous worst natural disasters in BC:

  • 1910 Rogers Pass avalanche.

  • 2003 Kootenay National Park forest fire.

  • 1975 Devastation Glacier landslide.

What happend?

Rogers pass avalanche

  • On March 4, 1910, an avalanche killed 62 men while they were clearing snow from the railway on a high mountain pass.

Kootenay National Park forest fire

  • A massive wildfire swept a destructive path through Kootenay National Park in 2003v

Devastation Glacier landslide

  • The Devastation Glacier landslide was a massive rock avalanche that originated from Devastation Glacier.
